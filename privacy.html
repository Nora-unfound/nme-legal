Privacy Policy

N=Me App · Last updated: May 2026

N=Me ("we", "our", "the app") is a personal health tracking app. This Privacy Policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

N=Me collects only the data you actively provide or authorize:

  • Account information — Your name and email address, obtained when you sign in with Apple (optional).
  • Apple User ID — A unique identifier provided by Apple Sign In to link your account across devices.
  • Health data — Steps, heart rate, sleep, HRV, and other HealthKit metrics you authorize. This data is read from the Apple Health app on your device.
  • Location — Your approximate location, recorded only while the app is open, to log environmental context alongside your health entries.
  • Habit and lifestyle records — Data you manually enter into the app (e.g., mood, energy, custom items you create).

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To power the core features of the app: trend analysis, correlation discovery, and personal experiments.
  • To sync your data across your own devices via iCloud (if you have iCloud enabled).
  • We do not use your data for advertising, marketing, or analytics.
  • We do not sell your data to any third party.

3. Data Storage

All your data is stored:

  • Locally on your device — in a private SQLite database.
  • In your personal iCloud account — via Apple CloudKit (private database), if you have iCloud enabled. Only you can access this data.

N=Me does not operate any external servers that store your personal data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with any third parties. Your health and lifestyle data never leaves your device and iCloud account.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Apple HealthKit — used to read health metrics. Governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple CloudKit — used for private iCloud sync. Governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple Sign In — optional, used to link an account identifier. Governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • App Store In-App Purchases — subscription payments are processed by Apple. We do not see or store payment details.

6. Your Rights

  • Access your data — All your data is visible within the app.
  • Export your data — Use Settings → Data Management → Export Data to download a full copy.
  • Delete your data — Use Profile → Delete Account to permanently erase all your data from the device and iCloud. This cannot be undone.
  • Revoke Health access — Go to iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → N=Me to remove access at any time.
  • Revoke Location access — Go to iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → N=Me.

7. Children's Privacy

N=Me is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
